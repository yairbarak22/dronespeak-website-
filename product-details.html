How does the AI understand natural language commands?

DroneSpeak uses advanced NLP models trained specifically on aviation and mission planning terminology. Our AI can parse complex, multi-step commands and translate them into precise flight instructions with 95% accuracy.

Yes! DroneSpeak works with 85% of commercial drones including DJI Enterprise series, ArduPilot/PX4 systems, and Autel Robotics. We provide integration support for custom setups.

What about regulatory compliance?

DroneSpeak is designed with compliance in mind. We support FAA Part 107 regulations, EASA standards, and provide automated flight plan approval workflows for restricted airspace.

How quickly can I get started?

Most customers are operational within 30 days. This includes drone integration, team training, and initial mission setup. Our professional services team handles the entire deployment process.
